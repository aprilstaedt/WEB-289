The History of Forsyth Family Practice Center

Forsyth Family Practice Center was opened in 1995 by Dr. Jonathan Littleton in Kernersville, NC. At that time, Dr. Littleton employed one receptionist, one nurse and one billing clerk.

Forsyth Family Practice Center expanded in 1998, when Dr. Laura Smith joined the practice. At this time, additional staff was also hired, and the patient base expanded from 100 to 250 patients. As the patient base grew, the facility moved to a new location and expanded the hours of operation to include a Saturday morning clinic.

Between 2000 and 2010, Forsyth Family Practice Center continued to grow, adding additional medical personnel and staff. In 2010, Dr. Littleton and the executive committee decided it was time to open a second office in Winston-Salem and expand the staff.

After experiencing a successful expansion, another expansion in Mount Airy was completed in early spring 2016.
